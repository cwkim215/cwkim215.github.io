About Me

Hi! I'm Christian Kim. I am currently a Junior at the University of Southern California studying Computer Science Games in the Viterbi School of Engineering. My passion for games started at a very young age, with my first ever action with my first ever laptop being downloading League of Legends. Now, I enjoy designing and programming them!

As a game designer/programmer, I want to create games that really focus on the player and give them an immersive expirience they won't be able to forget. I believe that playcentric design is the key to finding success in a game, so I want to keep that my primary focus as I head into my future projects/endeavors. However, my love for Computer Science and coding is not limited to games. I also really enjoy working on web dev and iOS projects as well. I believe that all I have learned and expiramented with (coding C++ data structures, web development, iOS projects, and game development) can help me no matter what I am doing.
