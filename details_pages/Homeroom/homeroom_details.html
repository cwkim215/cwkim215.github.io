Homeroom

Concept

Homeroom is a website project created with the idea of giving professors and students a way to interact through an online Q&A system. In addition, the website features an office hour queue in which the students can join to meet their instructors face-to-face or in a virtual meeting.

Implementation

    Frontend: HTML/CSS

    Backend: Java, SQL (MySQL)
