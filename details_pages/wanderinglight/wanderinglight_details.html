Wandering Light

Background

“Wandering Light” is an interactive narrative 2D game for mobile, where you play as a young girl that must complete tasks for the people-eating witch Baba Yaga in order to escape the dark forest. With only a small lantern & a strong will to guide her, players will explore the story and journey deeper into the forest by using the lantern to interact with the scene on the page. Shine through the dark, reveal hidden messages, and more as you wander through the forgotten tales of the forest.

Lantern Mechanic

My job for this project was being part of the programming team. Most of the coding work came through the lantern mechanic. The main idea for the lantern was that the whole scene would be dark until the lantern was able to shine light on an interactable object. From here, players could make decisions on which path they wanted to progress the story on. To make the lantern easy to use for the players, we made a click and drag type system and also gave it a brighter shine once a player got closer to an interactable object.

Skills and Completed Work

  • Created transitions in and out of scenes.
  • Implemented the full lantern mechanic.
  • Implemented a dialogue system for the game.
